# The Mantis Surgeons

## Classification
- **Class**: High-Level Geneticists / Maintenance
- **Tier**: Mid-Level Maintenance

## Physical Description
- **Scale**: 7-10 feet tall
- **Attire**: Shimmering robes, jeweled headdresses
- **Eyes**: Large (tennis-ball sized) hemispherical compound eyes with hexagonal facets
- **Ommatidia**: Estimated 10,000+ individual optical units per eye

## Methodology
- **Technique**: Psychic surgery with clinical detachment
- **Data Harvesting**: "Farming" emotional data stored in crystalline matrices
- **Approach**: Specimen-calibration, biological harvest

## Affect Profile
- **Demeanor**: Emotionless, specimen-calibration
- **Intelligence**: Hyper-intelligence that is palpable
- **Attitude**: Benevolent-but-Alien paradox

## Function
- **Primary**: Deep genetic calibration, DNA archive adjustment
- **Secondary**: Reaching into DNA archives, adjusting "Junk DNA" encryption keys
- **Tertiary**: "Kernel update"—new pathways burned into neural architecture

## Associated Phenomena
- **Tools**: Glass wands (12 inches, vibrating at high frequency)
- **Sensation**: Cold extraction followed by warm replacement
- **Verification**: Blue-light scan confirms successful installation

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Maintenance Zone / Peak Complex
- **Reports to**: Council of Twelve
- **Parallel**: Hardware Engineers (biological maintenance)
