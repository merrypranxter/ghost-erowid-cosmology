# The Zipper / The Unzipping

## Classification
- **Type**: Visual/Dimensional Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Salvia Onset

## Description
The Zipper is the visual phenomenon of reality appearing to unzip, peel apart, or open like a zipper, revealing what lies beneath or behind.

## Characteristics
- **Visual**: Reality splitting along a line
- **Motion**: Opening like zipper, peeling like fruit
- **Revelation**: Shows what is behind/beneath
- **Sound**: Zipping sound, peeling sound

## Variations
- **The Zipper**: Linear opening
- **The Peel**: Layer being removed
- **The Curtain**: Pulling back to reveal
- **The Page**: Turning to new view

## What Is Revealed
- **Behind**: Another layer of reality
- **Beneath**: The mechanism, the substrate
- **Within**: Inner workings of existence
- **Beyond**: Other dimensions, other spaces

## Function
- **Primary**: Reveal hidden layers
- **Secondary**: Access to other dimensions
- **Tertiary**: Challenge surface reality

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Fold**: Space folding
- **The Page**: Reality as book
- **The Membrane**: Thin layer between worlds
- **The Wheel**: Mechanism revealed
