# The Fractal City / The Geometric Metropolis

## Classification
- **Type**: Constructed Space
- **Layer**: Pattern Dimension

## Description
The Fractal City is a vast urban landscape built entirely of geometric patterns—every building, street, and inhabitant is composed of mathematical structures in infinite recursion.

## Characteristics
- **Architecture**: Buildings are crystalline geometric forms
- **Streets**: Tessellated patterns flowing through space
- **Inhabitants**: Geometric beings, pattern-creatures
- **Scale**: Infinite in all directions
- **Detail**: Infinite complexity at every scale

## Districts
- **The Crystal Quarter**: Pure crystalline structures
- **The Tessellation District**: Repeating tile patterns
- **The Fractal Heights**: Vertical infinite recursion
- **The Mandala Center**: Circular sacred space

## Associated Entities
- Geometric Architects, Pattern Beings, Light Beings

## Function
- **Primary**: Demonstrate mathematical beauty
- **Secondary**: Teach through geometric revelation
- **Tertiary**: Creative inspiration

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Pattern Realm
- **Function**: Mathematical manifestation
- **Parallel**: Platonic city, celestial Jerusalem
