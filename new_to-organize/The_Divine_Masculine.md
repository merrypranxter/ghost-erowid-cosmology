# The Divine Masculine / Yang Principle

## Classification
- **Type**: Cosmological Archetype
- **Domain**: Sacred Duality

## Core Concept
The Divine Masculine is the active, outward-moving principle of creation—associated with light, consciousness, structure, and manifestation.

## Characteristics
- **Active**: Doing, creating, manifesting
- **Outward**: Extending, projecting
- **Light**: Illumination, awareness
- **Structure**: Form, order, logic
- **Consciousness**: Awareness, knowing

## Manifestations
- **The Sun**: Source of light and life
- **The Father**: Protector, provider
- **The King**: Ruler, order-bringer
- **The Warrior**: Protector, defender
- **The Magician**: Transformer, alchemist
