# Salvia Divinorum Dosage Guide

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Reference
- **Domain**: Practical Information

## Threshold (0.1-0.25 g dried leaf)
- **Effects**: Subtle mood change, mild visuals
- **Duration**: 5-15 minutes
- **Use**: Microdosing, sensitivity testing

## Light (0.25-0.5 g dried leaf)
- **Effects**: Notable body sensations, mild dissociation
- **Duration**: 5-20 minutes
- **Use**: Beginner exploration

## Moderate (0.5-1 g dried leaf)
- **Effects**: Strong dissociation, entity contact possible
- **Duration**: 10-30 minutes
- **Use**: Standard experience

## Strong (1-2 g dried leaf)
- **Effects**: Intense dissociation, breakthrough likely
- **Duration**: 15-45 minutes
- **Use**: Experienced practitioners

## Heavy (2+ g dried leaf or extract)
- **Effects**: Complete dissolution, ego death
- **Duration**: 20-60 minutes
- **Use**: Advanced, with preparation

## Extract Potency
- **5x**: 5 times stronger than leaf
- **10x**: 10 times stronger
- **20x**: 20 times stronger
- **40x+**: Extremely potent, use caution

## Administration Methods
- **Smoking**: Most common, immediate onset
- **Quidding**: Chewing fresh leaves, traditional
- **Tincture**: Sublingual, gradual onset
- **Tea**: Less effective, milder

## Safety Considerations
- **Sitter**: Essential, especially for extracts
- **Setting**: Safe, comfortable, clear space
- **Physical**: Movements can be uncontrolled
- **Duration**: Short but intense
- **Integration**: Can be challenging

## Legal Status
- **Varies**: Illegal in some jurisdictions
- **Research**: Know local laws
- **Respect**: Traditional sacred plant
