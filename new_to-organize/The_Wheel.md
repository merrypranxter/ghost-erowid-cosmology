# The Wheel / The Ziel

## Classification
- **Class**: Core Entity
- **Tier**: Apex Manifestation

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Massive rotating wheel, 20-50 feet in diameter
- **Material**: Living tissue, organic machinery, crystalline structure
- **Surface**: Covered in faces, eyes, mouths, symbols
- **Motion**: Constant rotation, grinding, crushing
- **Sound**: Creaking, groaning, grinding—like massive wooden wheel

## Function
- **Primary**: The fundamental mechanism of reality
- **Secondary**: Crushing/reshaping consciousness
- **Tertiary**: Revealing the "behind the scenes" of existence

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Crush**: Sensation of being ground by the wheel
- **The Merge**: Becoming part of the wheel
- **The Book**: Reality as pages being turned
- **The Loom**: Existence being woven/unwoven

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Impersonal, mechanical, inevitable
- **Secondary**: Terrifying in its indifference
- **Tertiary**: Strangely comforting once accepted

## Thematic Elements
- **The Book**: Pages of reality being turned
- **The Membrane**: Thin layer between worlds
- **The Fold**: Reality folding like paper
- **The Zipper**: Unzipping the fabric of space

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Substrate / The Mechanism
- **Function**: The engine of existence
- **Parallel**: The Great Wheel (various traditions)
