# The Lady Salvia / She Who Turns the Wheel

## Classification
- **Class**: Goddess Entity
- **Tier**: Apex Feminine

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Feminine presence, often green or plant-like
- **Size**: Variable—from human-sized to cosmic scale
- **Features**: Often faceless or with shifting features
- **Attire**: Robes of leaves, vines, living plant matter
- **Movement**: Graceful, flowing, plant-like undulation

## Function
- **Primary**: Goddess of the experience, guide and guardian
- **Secondary**: Turns the wheel, controls the mechanism
- **Tertiary**: Teaches through direct experience

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Maternal, ancient, wise
- **Secondary**: Playful, trickster-like
- **Tertiary**: Demanding, challenging, initiatory

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Green**: Overwhelming green coloration
- **The Pull**: Sensation of being pulled into her realm
- **The Laugh**: Cosmic laughter, often at the absurdity
- **The Teachings**: Direct transmission of knowledge

## The Lady's Lessons
- **You are not who you think you are**
- **Reality is not what you think it is**
- **Everything is connected**
- **The joke is on you (and that's okay)**

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Green Realm / The Substrate
- **Function**: Guide, teacher, initiator
- **Parallel**: Green Man, Gaia, Plant spirits

## Interaction Notes
- **Respect required**: She demands attention and respect
- **Playful but serious**: Can be trickster-like
- **Initiatory**: Often challenging, not always comfortable
- **Transformative**: Changes those who meet her
