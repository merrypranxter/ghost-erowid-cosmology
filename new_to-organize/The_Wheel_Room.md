# The Wheel Room / The Mechanism Chamber

## Classification
- **Type**: Mechanism Space
- **Layer**: Salvia Core

## Description
The Wheel Room is the chamber where the Great Wheel turns—a vast space dominated by the massive turning mechanism that grinds and reshapes reality.

## Characteristics
- **Scale**: Cathedral-sized, dominated by Wheel
- **Sound**: Creaking, grinding, mechanical
- **Motion**: Constant rotation
- **Material**: Wood, metal, organic machinery
- **Atmosphere**: Heavy, inevitable, powerful

## Features
- **The Wheel**: Central mechanism, 20-50 feet diameter
- **The Gears**: Massive interlocking components
- **The Faces**: Carved on wheel surface
- **The Grinders**: Crushing, reshaping elements

## Associated Entities
- The Wheel, The Grinders, Lady Salvia

## Function
- **Primary**: Mechanism of transformation
- **Secondary**: Death and rebirth
- **Tertiary**: Teaching through crushing

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Mechanism
- **Function**: Transformation engine
- **Parallel**: Mills of God, wheels of karma
