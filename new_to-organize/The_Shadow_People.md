# The Shadow People / The Dark Ones

## Classification
- **Class**: Shadow Entities
- **Tier**: Varies

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Humanoid silhouettes, dark figures
- **Material**: Shadow, smoke, darkness
- **Features**: Often indistinct, vague
- **Eyes**: Sometimes glowing, red or white
- **Movement**: Jerky, unnatural, teleporting

## Function
- **Primary**: Manifestations of fear, paranoia
- **Secondary**: Testers of courage
- **Tertiary**: Revealers of shadow aspects

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Terrifying, threatening
- **Secondary**: Mysterious, watching
- **Tertiary**: Sometimes neutral, observing

## Types
- **The Watchers**: Stand and observe
- **The Approachers**: Move closer, threatening
- **The Hiders**: Lurk in corners, periphery
- **The Mimics**: Copy movements, mock

## Associated Phenomena
- **Paranoia**: Feeling of being watched, followed
- **Delusions**: False beliefs about their nature
- **Fear response**: Intense terror, panic
- **Shadow work**: Confronting dark aspects of psyche

## Coping Strategies
- **Acknowledge**: Don't fight, observe
- **Breathe**: Stay calm, breathe through fear
- **Remember**: They cannot truly harm
- **Integrate**: Learn from the encounter

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Shadow Realm
- **Function**: Shadow manifestation, fear testing
- **Parallel**: Shadow archetype, trickster
