# DMT Dosage Guide

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Reference
- **Domain**: Practical Information

## Threshold Dose (2-5 mg)
- **Effects**: Subtle mood lift, slight visuals
- **Duration**: 5-10 minutes
- **Use**: Microdosing, testing sensitivity

## Light Dose (5-10 mg)
- **Effects**: Visual patterns, mild body load
- **Duration**: 5-15 minutes
- **Use**: Beginner exploration

## Common Dose (10-20 mg)
- **Effects**: Strong visuals, entity contact possible
- **Duration**: 10-20 minutes
- **Use**: Standard breakthrough attempt

## Strong Dose (20-40 mg)
- **Effects**: Breakthrough likely, intense experience
- **Duration**: 15-30 minutes
- **Use**: Experienced practitioners

## Heavy Dose (40+ mg)
- **Effects**: Complete dissolution, ego death
- **Duration**: 20-45 minutes
- **Use**: Advanced, with preparation

## Administration Methods
- **Vaporization**: Most efficient, immediate onset
- **Smoking**: Traditional, harsh on lungs
- **Changa**: DMT-infused herbs, smoother
- **Injection**: Most potent, not recommended
- **Oral (with MAOI)**: Ayahuasca, longer duration

## Safety Considerations
- **Set and setting**: Crucial for positive experience
- **Sitter**: Recommended, especially for beginners
- **Heart conditions**: Avoid
- **Medications**: Check interactions
- **Mental health**: Caution with history of psychosis

## Integration
- **Journaling**: Record immediately after
- **Rest**: Allow time to process
- **Support**: Discuss with trusted person
- **Meditation**: Deepen understanding
