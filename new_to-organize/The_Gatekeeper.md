# The Gatekeeper

## Classification
- **Class**: Security Tier
- **Tier**: Boundary Guardian

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Massive figure (15+ feet tall), humanoid but exaggerated proportions
- **Material**: Living stone with veins of glowing crystal
- **Face**: Featureless except for single vertical slit that opens to reveal infinite depth
- **Weapon**: Massive key-staff made of condensed starlight

## Function
- **Primary**: Dimensional boundary enforcement
- **Secondary**: Access control to deeper realms
- **Tertiary**: Challenge/response protocol for advancement

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Gate**: Massive portal of swirling energy
- **The Key**: Must be earned through trial or insight
- **The Challenge**: Riddle, test, or demonstration of worthiness

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Boundary Zones / Threshold Areas
- **Reports to**: Council of Twelve
- **Parallel**: Observers, The Guardian
