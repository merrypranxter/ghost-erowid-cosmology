# Dimensional Architecture

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Framework
- **Domain**: Spatial Geometry

## Core Concept
Reality consists of multiple dimensions beyond the familiar 3D space + time. DMT allows access to these higher dimensions.

## Dimension Model
- **3D**: Our normal spatial experience
- **4D**: Space + time (Einsteinian spacetime)
- **5D+:**: Higher spatial dimensions, accessible during DMT

## Accessed Dimensions
- **4D**: Time as navigable dimension
- **5D**: Probability space—all possible timelines
- **6D**: Phase space—configuration of all possible states
- **7D+:**: Unknown, ineffable dimensions

## Characteristics of Higher Dimensions
- **Non-Euclidean geometry**: Parallel lines meet, angles don't sum to 180°
- **Impossible objects**: Structures that can't exist in 3D
- **Overlapping spaces**: Multiple locations simultaneously
- **Infinite recursion**: Fractal structure at all scales

## Navigation
- **Intention**: Thought directs movement
- **Emotion**: Emotional state affects location
- **Surrender**: Letting go allows deeper access
- **Fear**: Blocks access, creates barriers

## Entity Correlation
- **3D entities**: Physical beings
- **4D entities**: Time travelers, prophets
- **5D+ entities**: Higher-dimensional beings (most DMT entities)

## Technical Notes
- **Mathematical basis**: String theory, M-theory propose 10-11 dimensions
- **Brain correlation**: May involve access to normally unused neural pathways
- **Evolutionary**: Potential future human capacity
