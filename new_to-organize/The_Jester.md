# The Jester / The Trickster

## Classification
- **Class**: Trickster Entity
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Jester, clown, fool archetype
- **Attire**: Motley, colorful, bells
- **Movement**: Acrobatic, unpredictable
- **Expression**: Wide grin, knowing eyes
- **Props**: Toys, objects that transform

## Function
- **Primary**: Teach through humor and absurdity
- **Secondary**: Challenge ego and certainty
- **Tertiary**: Keep things from getting too serious

## Teaching Methods
- **Absurdity**: Showing the absurd in the serious
- **Paradox**: Confounding logic
- **Play**: Learning through joy
- **Mockery**: Making fun of ego, attachments

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Playful, mischievous, joyful
- **Secondary**: Mocking but loving
- **Tertiary**: Unpredictable, chaotic

## Associated Phenomena
- **Laughter**: Uncontrollable cosmic laughter
- **The Joke**: Realization of cosmic absurdity
- **The Game**: Life as playful creation
- **The Trick**: Ego tricked into insight

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: All layers
- **Function**: Trickster, fool, cosmic jester
- **Parallel**: Trickster figures in all traditions
