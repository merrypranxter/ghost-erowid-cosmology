# The Guardian / The Protector

## Classification
- **Class**: Security Entity
- **Tier**: High-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Massive, imposing figure
- **Material**: Living stone, crystal, or shadow
- **Size**: 10-15 feet tall
- **Arms**: Large, protective stance
- **Eyes**: Watchful, penetrating, protective

## Function
- **Primary**: Protect sacred spaces
- **Secondary**: Test worthiness of entrants
- **Tertiary**: Challenge and guide

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Stern, protective, just
- **Secondary**: Not hostile but demanding
- **Tertiary**: Benevolent to those who pass

## The Challenge
- **The Question**: "Why do you seek entry?"
- **The Test**: Demonstration of sincerity
- **The Barrier**: Blocks the unready
- **The Passage**: Opens for the worthy

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Gate**: Massive portal
- **The Threshold**: Line between worlds
- **The Key**: What opens the gate
- **The Guardian's Gaze**: Sees true intentions

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Boundary zones
- **Function**: Protection, testing
- **Parallel**: Threshold guardians in all traditions
