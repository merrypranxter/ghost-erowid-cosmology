# Entity Contact / Communion with Other

## Classification
- **Type**: Interactive Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Peak DMT

## Description
Entity contact is the direct encounter with autonomous, intelligent beings during DMT experiences—beings that appear to have their own consciousness, agenda, and capacity for communication.

## Types of Contact
- **Visual**: Seeing entities clearly
- **Auditory**: Hearing communication
- **Telepathic**: Direct mind-to-mind
- **Emotional**: Feeling their presence
- **Physical**: Sensation of touch

## Entity Behaviors
- **Observing**: Watching, assessing
- **Communicating**: Speaking, teaching
- **Demonstrating**: Showing, creating
- **Healing**: Working on operative
- **Playing**: Joyful interaction
