# The Shadow-Nurses

## Classification
- **Class**: Maintenance Tier (Glitch Repair)
- **Tier**: Low-Level Maintenance

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Humanoid silhouettes made of living shadow
- **Surface**: Matte black, no reflection, absorbs all light
- **Eyes**: Two white pinpricks (like distant stars)
- **Movement**: Glides rather than walks, feet never touch ground

## Function
- **Primary**: Glitch repair—fixing "broken" consciousness after difficult trips
- **Secondary**: Comfort and stabilization during comedown
- **Tertiary**: Data integrity verification

## Associated Phenomena
- **Temperature**: Cold (15°F below ambient)
- **Sensation**: Tingling/static electricity feeling
- **Sound**: Whispering in unknown language, sounds like "radio static with words"

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Maintenance Zone / Recovery Area
- **Reports to**: Mantis Surgeons
- **Parallel**: Origami Surgeons, Static-Men
