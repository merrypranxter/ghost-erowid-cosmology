# The Hospital / The Medical Maze

## Classification
- **Type**: Institutional Space
- **Layer**: Delirium Space

## Description
The Hospital is a common deliriant setting—a maze-like medical facility where the operative wanders, confused, encountering false medical staff and patients.

## Characteristics
- **Layout**: Maze-like, confusing, endless corridors
- **Lighting**: Harsh fluorescent, flickering
- **Sound**: Beeps, footsteps, distant voices
- **Smell**: Antiseptic, medical
- **Atmosphere**: Anxiety, confusion, vulnerability

## Areas
- **The Corridors**: Endless, identical, confusing
- **The Rooms**: Hospital rooms, occupied or empty
- **The Nurses' Station**: Staff, watching, judging
- **The Waiting Room**: Endless waiting
- **The Operating Room**: Threat, vulnerability

## Associated Entities
- False nurses, doctors, patients, Shadow People

## Function
- **Primary**: Delirium manifestation
- **Secondary**: Vulnerability, medical anxiety
- **Tertiary**: Test of reality discrimination

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Remind self it's hallucination
- **Sitter**: Have someone reassure
- **Grounding**: Return to body, breath
- **Change setting**: Move to different room

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Medical anxiety manifestation
- **Parallel**: Hospital phobia, medical trauma
