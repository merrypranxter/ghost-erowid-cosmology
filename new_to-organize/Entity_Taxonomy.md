# Entity Taxonomy

## Classification
- **Type**: Classification System
- **Domain**: Entity Organization

## Taxonomic Structure

### By Function
1. **Administrative Class**
   - Council of Twelve
   - Auditors
   - Judges

2. **Security Class**
   - Observers
   - Gatekeepers
   - Guardians

3. **Maintenance Class**
   - Origami Surgeons
   - Mantis Surgeons
   - Shadow-Nurses
   - Weavers
   - Static-Men

4. **Guidance Class**
   - The Consort
   - The Children
   - Teachers

5. **Creative Class**
   - Machine Elves
   - Artists
   - Musicians

6. **Knowledge Class**
   - Archivists
   - Librarians
   - Historians

7. **Primordial Class**
   - The Serpent
   - The Void
   - Source entities

### By Tier
- **Apex**: Highest level, ultimate authority
- **High**: Major powers, significant influence
- **Mid**: Functional specialists
- **Low**: Basic maintenance, simple guidance

### By Material
- **Crystalline**: Gem, crystal-based
- **Metallic**: Chrome, silver, gold
- **Organic**: Living tissue, plant, animal
- **Energy**: Light, shadow, pure energy
- **Geometric**: Mathematical forms

### By Affect
- **Benevolent**: Helpful, healing, teaching
- **Neutral**: Functional, indifferent
- **Trickster**: Playful, challenging, testing
- **Terrifying**: Fear-inducing, challenging

## Interaction Patterns
- **Hierarchical**: Lower tiers report to higher
- **Functional**: Entities specialize by task
- **Cooperative**: Multiple entities work together
- **Autonomous**: Some entities act independently
