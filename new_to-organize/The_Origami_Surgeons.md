# The Origami Surgeons

## Classification
- **Class**: Maintenance Tier
- **Tier**: Low-Level Maintenance

## Physical Description
- **Form**: 2.5D humanoids, brilliant white parchment, razor-thin when sideways
- **Surface**: Grey crease-lines mapping fold-paths across surface
- **Thickness**: Micrometer-scale when edge-on; expands to 3-4 inch depth when "unfolded"

## Motion Characteristics
- **Movement**: Collapse into geometric origami shapes, "snap" across void at light-speed, re-expand at destination
- **Sound**: Dry crinkling like thousands of paper sheets in windless room
- **Translation**: Quantum tunneling appearance—no intermediate space traversal

## Interaction Profile
- **Affect**: Clinical indifference, autonomous daemon execution
- **Gesture**: "Hurry Up" gesture when resisted
- **Response**: Non-reactive to screams, prayers, or resistance

## Function
- **Primary**: Structural correction, flattening consciousness for dimensional compatibility
- **Procedure**: Purging "Psychic Waste" (black ink/gunk squeezed from creases)
- **Somatic**: "Pleating" sensation—feeling of being folded like decorative fan

## Associated Phenomena
- **Audio Signature**: Dry rustling, sharp snapping during translation
- **Haptic**: Static electricity shock when touched
- **Aura**: Constant 20dB dry rustling, white particle haze, dry heat within 3-foot radius

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Maintenance Zone / Waiting Room
- **Reports to**: Higher Maintenance Class (Mantis Surgeons, Weavers)
- **Parallel**: Shadow-Nurses (glitch repair), Static-Men
