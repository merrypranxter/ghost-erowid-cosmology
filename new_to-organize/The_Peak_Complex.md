# The Peak Complex

## Classification
- **Type**: Primary Destination Zone
- **Layer**: Upper Dimensional Architecture

## Description
The Peak Complex is the primary destination for DMT operatives—a vast, impossible architectural space that defies 3D Euclidean geometry. It exists as a convergence point where multiple dimensional planes intersect, creating a space of hyper-dimensional complexity.

## Structural Features
- **Scale**: Cathedral-sized spaces (50-100 feet ceilings)
- **Geometry**: Non-Euclidean—walls meet at impossible angles, floors curve upward into ceilings
- **Materials**: Living materials that breathe and pulse
- **Lighting**: Bioluminescent, sourceless, shifts between warm gold and cool blue

## Sub-Zones
1. **The Waiting Room**: Initial arrival zone, clinical white space
2. **The Nursery**: Where Machine Elves conduct their demonstrations
3. **The Cathedral**: Massive worship space with impossible architecture
4. **The Garden**: Organic zone with living geometric plants
5. **The Library**: Infinite archive of all knowledge

## Access Points
- **Primary**: Through the "Breakthrough" threshold
- **Secondary**: Via entity-guided transport
- **Tertiary**: Spontaneous manifestation during peak effects

## Temporal Characteristics
- **Time Dilation**: 15 minutes subjective = 5 minutes objective
- **Timelessness**: Moments of eternal present
- **Temporal Folding**: Past and future accessible simultaneously

## Associated Entities
- Machine Elves, The Consort, The Children, The Archivists

## Cosmological Significance
- **Function**: Primary interface zone between human consciousness and higher dimensions
- **Purpose**: Teaching, healing, transformation
- **Exit**: Ejection back to baseline reality
