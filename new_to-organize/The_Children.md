# The Children / The Innocents

## Classification
- **Class**: Guidance Class
- **Tier**: Low-Level Guidance

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Child-like figures, 3-4 feet tall
- **Features**: Large eyes, small mouths, gender-neutral
- **Material**: Translucent, glowing softly from within
- **Clothing**: Simple robes that shift color based on mood

## Behavior
- **Affect**: Playful, curious, non-judgmental
- **Interaction**: Want to show things, share secrets
- **Communication**: Telepathic, simple concepts, pure emotion

## Function
- **Primary**: Innocence preservation
- **Secondary**: Play as learning mechanism
- **Tertiary**: Emotional healing through childlike wonder

## Associated Phenomena
- **Games**: Reality becomes playground
- **Toys**: Impossible objects that teach through play
- **Laughter**: Contagious joy that dissolves fear

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Peak Complex / The Nursery
- **Reports to**: None (autonomous)
- **Parallel**: Machine Elves (playful aspect)
