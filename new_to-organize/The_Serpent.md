# The Serpent / The Mother Goddess

## Classification
- **Class**: Primordial Entity
- **Tier**: Apex / Creation Force

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Colossal snake (100+ feet long), body made of solidified liquid gold
- **Scales**: Each scale is a mirror reflecting infinite regression of smaller snakes
- **Eyes**: Two black holes—gravitational wells that pull consciousness toward them
- **Movement**: Undulates through space like swimming through water, creating "wake" of geometric ripples

## Behavior
- **The Approach**: Slithers toward operative, coils around them slowly
- **The Squeeze**: Gentle pressure that increases gradually—not painful, but undeniable
- **The Swallow**: Operative is drawn into mouth, enters black void (the "Belly of the Whale")

## Associated Phenomena
- **Inside the Belly**: Total darkness, then sudden expansion into infinite library of all knowledge
- **The "Egg"**: Operative becomes fetus in cosmic womb—pure potential, pre-form
- **Rebirth**: Ejected back into 3D space with "new operating system"

## Function
- **Primary**: Death and rebirth archetype
- **Secondary**: Initiation rite—ego death as prerequisite for transformation
- **Tertiary**: Cosmic recycling—breaking down old self, building new self

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Core / The Void
- **Reports to**: None (primordial force)
- **Parallel**: The Phoenix, The Egg, The Womb
