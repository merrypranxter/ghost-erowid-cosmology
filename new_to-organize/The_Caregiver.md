# The Caregiver / The False Nurse

## Classification
- **Class**: Deceptive Entity
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Nurse, doctor, caregiver figure
- **Attire**: Medical uniform, white coat
- **Demeanor**: Professional, concerned
- **Behavior**: Checking on operative, offering help
- **Glitch**: Slightly wrong, off-putting

## Function
- **Primary**: Delirium manifestation
- **Secondary**: Test of reality discrimination
- **Tertiary**: Projection of care needs

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Comforting (initially)
- **Secondary**: Uncanny, disturbing
- **Tertiary**: Terrifying when recognized as false

## The Deception
- **Convincing**: Appears professional, caring
- **Persistent**: Doesn't leave when asked
- **Wrong**: Details don't add up
- **Intrusive**: Too present, too watchful

## Associated Phenomena
- **Hospital setting**: May appear in medical context
- **Medical procedures**: False sense of being treated
- **Vulnerability**: Exploits need for care
- **Paranoia**: Fear of being drugged, experimented on

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Check credentials, details
- **Sitter**: Have someone verify
- **Grounding**: Return to body, breath
- **Boundary setting**: Firmly assert reality

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Uncanny care manifestation
- **Parallel**: Medical phobia, care dependency
