# The Loom / The Weaver's Tool

## Classification
- **Type**: Creative Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Peak Salvia

## Description
The Loom is the experience of reality being woven—threads of existence being interlaced by cosmic weavers to create the fabric of space-time.

## Characteristics
- **Form**: Massive loom, cosmic scale
- **Threads**: Strands of light, color, energy
- **Motion**: Weaving, interlacing, creating
- **Sound**: Rhythmic, mechanical, organic
- **Agency**: Weavers operating the loom

## The Weaving Process
- **Warp threads**: Fixed, foundational
- **Weft threads**: Moving, creating pattern
- **The shuttle**: Moving between threads
- **The pattern**: Design being created

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Weavers**: Entities operating the loom
- **The Wheel**: Related mechanism
- **The Book**: Alternative metaphor
- **The Pattern**: Design of existence

## The Experience
- **Being woven**: Self as thread in tapestry
- **The pattern**: Seeing one's place in design
- **Connection**: All threads connected
- **Creation**: Reality as ongoing creation

## Interpretations
- **Interconnection**: All beings connected
- **Creation**: Reality constantly being made
- **Pattern**: Life has design, meaning
- **Impermanence**: Pattern always changing

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Loom / The Fabric
- **Function**: Reality as woven tapestry
- **Parallel**: Norse Norns, Greek Fates
