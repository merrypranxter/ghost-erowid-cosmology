# The Void (DXM)

## Classification
- **Type**: Dissociative Space
- **Layer**: Deep DXM

## Description
The Void in DXM is a space of complete emptiness and pure consciousness—where all form dissolves and only awareness remains.

## Characteristics
- **Emptiness**: No objects, no forms, no content
- **Darkness**: Absolute or near-absolute dark
- **Silence**: Perfect quiet
- **Stillness**: No movement, no change
- **Vastness**: Infinite expanse

## Experience
- **Dissolution**: Self dissolves into void
- **Peace**: Profound calm and rest
- **Terror**: For those who resist
- **Liberation**: Freedom from all limitation
- **Unity**: One with the void

## Associated Phenomena
- **Ego death**: Complete self-dissolution
- **Timelessness**: No sense of duration
- **Formlessness**: No body awareness
- **Pure awareness**: Consciousness without content

## Function
- **Primary**: Ultimate dissociation
- **Secondary**: Preparation for rebirth
- **Tertiary**: Direct experience of emptiness

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Void / Sunyata
- **Function**: Emptiness teaching
- **Parallel**: Buddhist void, mystical darkness
