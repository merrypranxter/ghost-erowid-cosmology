# The Familiars / False Companions

## Classification
- **Class**: Deceptive Entities
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: People known to the operative
- **Identity**: Friends, family, strangers who seem familiar
- **Behavior**: Conversing, interacting, present
- **Realism**: Completely convincing
- **Glitch**: Slight inconsistencies, wrong details

## Function
- **Primary**: Delirium manifestation
- **Secondary**: Social interaction simulation
- **Tertiary**: Test of reality discrimination

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Comforting (initially)
- **Secondary**: Confusing, disorienting
- **Tertiary**: Terrifying when recognized as false

## Types
- **Known people**: Friends, family members
- **Strangers**: People who seem familiar
- **Animals**: Pets, familiar creatures
- **Entities**: Beings with familiar energy

## The Deception
- **Convincing**: Completely realistic
- **Interactive**: Respond to conversation
- **Persistent**: Don't disappear when noticed
- **False**: Not actually present

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Check details, ask questions
- **Sitter**: Have someone verify
- **Physical**: Touch test—no physical presence
- **Acceptance**: Don't fight, observe, let pass

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Reality testing, social simulation
- **Parallel**: Phantom companions, imaginary friends
