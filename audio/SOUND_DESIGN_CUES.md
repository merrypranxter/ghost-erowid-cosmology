# Sound Design Cues — Psychedelic OS TikTok Pack

Use these as: background audio notes, Foley ideas, or what to search in sound libraries.

---

## Carrier Wave (THR_CARRIER_WAVE)
- rising sine tone + subtle tremolo
- faint electrical shimmer
- spectrogram “tightening” feel
- optional: soft heartbeat underlay

## Pop / Snap (THR_POP)
- single click + white-noise burst
- short “camera shutter” feel
- micro reverb tail

## Zipper / Fold (THR_ZIPPER)
- cloth rip / zipper pull
- tape peel sound
- airy suction “membrane opening”

## Tunnel Vortex (GEO_TUNNEL)
- Doppler whoosh loop
- accelerating wind + rotating phase

## Hat Man / Terror Edge (ENT_HATMAN + EMO_TERROR_EDGE)
- sub-bass pulse (not too loud)
- distant metal creak
- jittery glitch ticks
- silence drop for 0.2s before alert banner

## Benevolence (EMO_BENEVOLENCE / ENT_GAIA)
- warm pad drone
- soft choir-like shimmer (non-lyrical)
- gentle bell sparkle

## Nostalgia (EMO_NOSTALGIA)
- VHS hum
- cassette warble
- soft room tone + gentle tape hiss

## Trash Cache / Corruption (LOC_TRASH_CACHE)
- buffer stutter
- digital clipping “crumbles”
- UI error beeps layered with static

## 3D Language (GEO_3D_LANGUAGE)
- vocal-formant synth
- syllable pops turned into percussion
- whispered consonants as texture
