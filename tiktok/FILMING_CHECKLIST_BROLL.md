# Filming Checklist + B-Roll Shotlist (TikTok)

## A) Talking-Head Checklist
- Open with 1 sentence hook (from `tiktok/SCRIPT_HOOKS_20.md`)
- Show 1 notification overlay immediately
- Keep to 1 motif per video (unless it’s “stack” day)
- End with 1 question CTA

## B) B-Roll Shot Ideas (low effort, high vibe)
- Close-up of screen showing scrolling “syslog” text
- Hand hovering over “loading bar” graphic
- Finger tapping “ACKNOWLEDGE” button
- Slow pan across printed “Underlayer Stack” poster mock (even digital)
- Quick cuts of:
  - hex grid overlay
  - fractal zoom
  - tunnel vortex
  - zipper seam peel
  - red alert banner
- Over-the-shoulder “creating prompts” shot
- Notebook scribbles: “carrier wave / pop / zipper / void”
- Glitch transitions synced to audio (tap, snap, peel)

## C) Episode Assembly Formula (repeatable)
1) Hook line (0–2s)
2) Notification overlay appears (2–4s)
3) Explain motif (4–20s)
4) Show loop visual (20–30s)
5) CTA question (last 3s)

## D) On-Screen Text Rules (readability)
- 6–10 words max per line
- Keep headline ALL CAPS
- Microtext 1–2 lines
- Use red only for ALERT / SECURITY
- Use whiteout minimal for BIOS/VOID
