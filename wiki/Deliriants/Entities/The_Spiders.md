# The Spiders / Arachnid Manifestations

## Classification
- **Class**: Insectoid Entities
- **Tier**: Low-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Spiders of all sizes
- **Size**: From normal to gigantic (room-sized)
- **Number**: Single to swarms covering everything
- **Movement**: Crawling, scurrying, dropping
- **Realism**: Hyper-realistic, indistinguishable from real

## Function
- **Primary**: Fear manifestation
- **Secondary**: Delirium indicator
- **Tertiary**: Test of reality discrimination

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Terrifying for arachnophobes
- **Secondary**: Disgusting, creepy
- **Tertiary**: Sometimes neutral (for spider lovers)

## Associated Phenomena
- **Formication**: Sensation of bugs crawling on skin
- **Visual hallucinations**: Seeing spiders everywhere
- **Tactile hallucinations**: Feeling them crawl
- **Delusions**: Believing they are real

## Common Manifestations
- **On walls**: Crawling across surfaces
- **On skin**: Sensation of crawling
- **In corners**: Lurking, watching
- **From above**: Dropping from ceiling

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Remind self it's hallucination
- **Don't scratch**: Avoid skin damage
- **Change setting**: Move to different room
- **Sitter**: Have someone reassure

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Fear manifestation, reality test
- **Parallel**: Common phobia manifestation
