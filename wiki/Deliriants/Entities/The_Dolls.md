# The Dolls / The Mannequins

## Classification
- **Class**: False Entities
- **Tier**: Low-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Dolls, mannequins, puppets
- **Size**: Varies from small to life-sized
- **Features**: Painted faces, glass eyes, fixed expressions
- **Movement**: Jerky, puppet-like, unnatural
- **Material**: Porcelain, wood, plastic, cloth

## Function
- **Primary**: Uncanny valley manifestation
- **Secondary**: Test of reality discrimination
- **Tertiary**: Symbol of artificiality, falseness

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Creepy, unsettling, uncanny
- **Secondary**: Sad, pathetic
- **Tertiary**: Terrifying when moving

## Associated Phenomena
- **Uncanny valley**: Almost human but not quite
- **False life**: Appear alive but aren't
- **The puppeteer**: Sense of being controlled
- **The collection**: Many dolls, watching

## Types
- **Porcelain dolls**: Classic creepy dolls
- **Mannequins**: Store dummies, posed
- **Puppets**: String-controlled, jerky
- **Clowns**: Related uncanny figure

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Uncanny manifestation
- **Parallel**: Dolls in horror, uncanny valley

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Remind self they're hallucinations
- **Don't engage**: Avoid interaction
- **Change setting**: Move to different space
- **Sitter support**: Have someone reassure
