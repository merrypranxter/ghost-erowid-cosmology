# The Hat Man

## Classification
- **Class**: Shadow Entity (Specific)
- **Tier**: High-Level Shadow

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Tall male figure in hat and coat
- **Height**: 6-8 feet tall
- **Attire**: Fedora or similar hat, long coat
- **Face**: Shadowed, indistinct, sometimes red eyes
- **Presence**: Heavy, oppressive, watching

## Function
- **Primary**: Shadow archetype manifestation
- **Secondary**: Fear trigger, paranoia source
- **Tertiary**: Shadow aspect of psyche

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Terrifying, oppressive
- **Secondary**: Mysterious, enigmatic
- **Tertiary**: Sometimes protective (rare)

## Associated Phenomena
- **Sleep paralysis**: Often appears during SP
- **Delirium**: Common in anticholinergic states
- **Shadow people**: Related phenomenon
- **Cultural**: Appears across cultures, times

## Theories
- **Archetype**: Universal shadow figure
- **Neural**: Hypnagogic hallucination
- **Paranormal**: Actual entity (speculative)
- **Psychological**: Manifestation of fear

## Coping Strategies
- **Don't engage**: Avoid eye contact, interaction
- **Stay calm**: Fear feeds the phenomenon
- **Ground**: Return to body, physical reality
- **Light**: Turn on lights, dispel shadows

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Shadow Realm
- **Function**: Shadow manifestation, fear testing
- **Parallel**: Boogeyman, shadow archetype
