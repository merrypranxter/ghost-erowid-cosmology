# The Insects / The Swarm

## Classification
- **Class**: Phobia Entities
- **Tier**: Low-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: All types of insects
- **Size**: Normal to exaggerated
- **Number**: Single to swarms covering everything
- **Movement**: Crawling, flying, scurrying
- **Sound**: Buzzing, clicking, rustling

## Function
- **Primary**: Phobia manifestation
- **Secondary**: Delirium indicator
- **Tertiary**: Test of reality discrimination

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Disgusting, terrifying
- **Secondary**: Overwhelming, invasive
- **Tertiary**: Panic-inducing

## Common Manifestations
- **Spiders**: Most common, all sizes
- **Ants**: Swarms, lines, columns
- **Flies**: Buzzing, landing on skin
- **Cockroaches**: Scurrying, hiding
- **Beetles**: Crawling, crunching

## Associated Phenomena
- **Formication**: Sensation of bugs on skin
- **Swarm behavior**: Coordinated movement
- **Infestation**: Sense of being overrun
- **Crawling sensation**: Physical feeling

## Coping Strategies
- **Reality testing**: Remind self it's hallucination
- **Don't scratch**: Avoid skin damage
- **Sitter**: Have someone verify not real
- **Change setting**: Move to different room
- **Grounding**: Focus on breath, body

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Delirium Space
- **Function**: Fear/phobia manifestation
- **Parallel**: Common phobia triggers
