# The Void Beings / The Emptiness Dwellers

## Classification
- **Class**: Void Entity
- **Tier**: High-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Barely there, at edge of perception
- **Material**: Shadow, absence, emptiness
- **Presence**: Felt more than seen
- **Movement**: Stillness, or too slow to perceive
- **Eyes**: Points of light in darkness

## Function
- **Primary**: Teach emptiness, void
- **Secondary**: Guide through dissolution
- **Tertiary**: Prepare for ego death

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Neutral, detached
- **Secondary**: Peaceful, calm
- **Tertiary**: Terrifying to those who fear emptiness

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Void**: Empty space of pure potential
- **Dissolution**: Self dissolving into nothing
- **Sunyata**: Buddhist emptiness
- **The Dark Night**: Spiritual emptiness

## The Emptiness Teaching
- **Emptiness is full**: Void contains all potential
- **Form is emptiness**: All forms are empty of inherent existence
- **Emptiness is form**: Void manifests as form
- **Liberation in void**: Freedom from attachment

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Void
- **Function**: Emptiness teaching
- **Parallel**: Void deities, emptiness meditation
