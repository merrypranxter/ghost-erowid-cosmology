# DXM Dosage Guide

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Reference
- **Domain**: Practical Information

## Threshold (60-100 mg)
- **Effects**: Subtle mood lift, slight stimulation
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours
- **Use**: Testing sensitivity

## First Plateau (100-200 mg)
- **Effects**: Mood lift, music enhancement
- **Duration**: 3-6 hours
- **Use**: Social, light dissociation

## Second Plateau (200-400 mg)
- **Effects**: Strong dissociation, robo-walk
- **Duration**: 4-8 hours
- **Use**: Standard dissociative experience

## Third Plateau (400-800 mg)
- **Effects**: Deep dissociation, out-of-body possible
- **Duration**: 6-12 hours
- **Use**: Experienced practitioners

## Fourth Plateau (800+ mg)
- **Effects**: Complete dissociation, ego death
- **Duration**: 8-24 hours
- **Use**: Advanced, with preparation

## Administration
- **Syrup**: Most common, contains other ingredients
- **Gel caps**: Pure DXM, no additives
- **Powder**: Pure DXM, precise dosing
- **Extract**: Concentrated, avoid other ingredients

## Important: Avoid Other Ingredients
- **Acetaminophen**: Liver damage risk
- **Antihistamines**: Add sedation, confusion
- **Decongestants**: Blood pressure issues
- **Guaifenesin**: Nausea, kidney stones

## Safety Considerations
- **Sitter**: Essential for 3rd and 4th plateau
- **Set and setting**: Important for positive experience
- **Nausea**: Common, have bucket ready
- **Itch**: Histamine release, antihistamine helps
- **Heart conditions**: Avoid
- **MAOIs**: Dangerous interaction
- **SSRIs**: Serotonin syndrome risk

## Body Load
- **Nausea**: Very common on come-up
- **Itch**: Histamine-related
- **Robo-walk**: Uncoordinated movement
- **Pupil dilation**: Light sensitivity

## Integration
- **Rest**: Allow time to recover
- **Hydration**: Drink water
- **Journaling**: Record insights
- **Afterglow**: Can last days, usually positive
