# The Plateaus / DXM Levels

## Classification
- **Type**: Dose-Dependent States
- **Domain**: DXM Experience Structure

## Description
The Plateaus are the distinct levels of DXM experience, each with characteristic effects, intensity, and qualitative differences.

## First Plateau (1.5-2.5 mg/kg)
- **Mood lift**: Euphoria, stimulation
- **Music enhancement**: Enhanced appreciation
- **Mild dissociation**: Slight distance
- **Social**: Can still interact
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours

## Second Plateau (2.5-7.5 mg/kg)
- **Strong dissociation**: Clear separation
- **Cognitive changes**: Altered thinking
- **Closed-eye visuals**: Patterned visuals
- **Robo-walk**: Uncoordinated movement
- **Duration**: 3-6 hours

## Third Plateau (7.5-15 mg/kg)
- **Deep dissociation**: Out-of-body possible
- **Profound visuals**: Complex scenes
- **Ego effects**: Significant ego dissolution
- **Flange**: Prominent auditory distortion
- **Duration**: 6-8 hours

## Fourth Plateau (15+ mg/kg)
- **Complete dissociation**: Out-of-body common
- **Ego death**: Complete dissolution
- **Mystical experiences**: Transcendent states
- **Danger zone**: Higher risk
- **Duration**: 8-12+ hours

## Sigma Plateau (Redosing)
- **Not a true plateau**: From repeated dosing
- **Unique effects**: Different from single dose
- **Psychotomimetic**: Can mimic psychosis
- **Not recommended**: Unpredictable, risky

## Plateau Navigation
- **Start low**: Learn your response
- **Respect each level**: Don't rush
- **Set and setting**: More important at higher levels
- **Sitter**: Essential for 3rd and 4th
