# The Dextroverse / DXM Space

## Classification
- **Type**: Dissociative Space
- **Layer**: DXM Dimension

## Description
The Dextroverse is the unique dimensional space accessed through DXM—a realm of dissociation where mind and body separate, and consciousness floats free.

## Characteristics
- **Dissociation**: Mind separate from body
- **Floating**: Weightless, drifting sensation
- **Vastness**: Infinite space, cosmic scale
- **Darkness**: Often dark, void-like
- **Machinery**: Mechanical, technological elements

## The Plateaus
- **1st Plateau**: Mild dissociation, stimulation
- **2nd Plateau**: Strong dissociation, floating
- **3rd Plateau**: Deep dissociation, out-of-body
- **4th Plateau**: Complete dissociation, ego death

## Sub-Locations
- **The Void**: Empty space of pure consciousness
- **The Machine Room**: Mechanical underpinnings
- **The Library**: Knowledge storage
- **The Playground**: Cosmic game space

## Associated Entities
- The Robots, The Dextroverse Guide, The Jester

## Function
- **Primary**: Dissociative exploration
- **Secondary**: Ego dissolution
- **Tertiary**: Insight through detachment

## Cosmological Position
