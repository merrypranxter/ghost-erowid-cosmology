# Time Dilation / Temporal Distortion

## Classification
- **Type**: Temporal Phenomenon
- **Phase**: All phases

## Description
Time dilation is the dramatic distortion of temporal perception during DMT experiences—subjective time expanding to fill what objectively is only minutes.

## Types
- **Expansion**: Subjective time longer than objective (most common)
- **Contraction**: Subjective time shorter than objective (rare)
- **Non-linearity**: Past, present, future accessible simultaneously
- **Eternity**: Sense of existing outside of time

## Ratios
- **Mild**: 2:1 (subjective:objective)
- **Moderate**: 5:1 to 10:1
- **Extreme**: 100:1 to infinite
- **Eternal**: No time at all—pure present

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Eternal Moment**: Single moment stretched to infinity
- **Life Review**: Entire life experienced in an instant
- **Future Preview**: Probabilistic futures accessible
- **Past Access**: Historical events as present experience

## Function
- **Primary**: Allows deep processing in short objective time
- **Secondary**: Access to normally inaccessible time scales
- **Tertiary**: Teaching about the nature of time

## Technical Notes
- **Neural basis**: May involve disruption of time-keeping brain regions
- **Quantum possibility**: May reflect actual temporal flexibility
- **Evolutionary**: Allows rapid learning and adaptation
