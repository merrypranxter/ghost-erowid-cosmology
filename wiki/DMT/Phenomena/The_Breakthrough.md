# The Breakthrough / Threshold Crossing

## Classification
- **Type**: Transitional Phenomenon
- **Phase**: DMT Onset

## Description
The Breakthrough is the threshold crossing from normal consciousness into the DMT space—a dramatic transition marked by the Chrysanthemum Mandala and a sense of passing through a barrier.

## Stages
1. **The Rush**: Rapid onset, heart acceleration
2. **The Hum**: Auditory tone increases
3. **The Mandala**: Chrysanthemum appears
4. **The Pressure**: Intense physical/mental pressure
5. **The Fear**: Existential terror before threshold
6. **The Surrender**: Letting go, accepting
7. **The Passage**: Moving through the center
8. **The Arrival**: Entering the DMT space

## Characteristics
- **Intensity**: Overwhelming, undeniable
- **Speed**: Happens in seconds
- **Fear**: Common, natural response
- **Beauty**: Mandala is stunningly beautiful
- **Force**: Feels like being pulled/pushed

## The Threshold
- **Barrier**: Sense of crossing something
- **Filter**: Not everyone breaks through
- **Surrender**: Required for passage
- **Resistance**: Blocks access, increases suffering

## Associated Phenomena
- **Chrysanthemum Mandala**: Gateway visual
- **The Hum**: Auditory carrier wave
- **Body load**: Physical sensations
- **Ego dissolution**: Self begins to dissolve

## Function
- **Primary**: Transition to DMT space
- **Secondary**: Filter—only ready pass
- **Tertiary**: Initiatory—prepares for experience

## Integration
- **Preparation**: Knowing what to expect helps
- **Surrender**: Key to smooth passage
- **Breath**: Focus on breathing
- **Trust**: The process is safe
