# The Chrysanthemum Mandala

## Classification
- **Type**: Visual Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Pre-Breakthrough

## Description
The Chrysanthemum Mandala is the iconic pre-breakthrough visual—a vast, rotating flower-like structure that serves as the gateway to deeper DMT spaces. It is both beautiful and terrifying in its complexity.

## Visual Characteristics
- **Form**: Massive flower with infinite petals
- **Colors**: Rainbow spectrum, constantly shifting
- **Movement**: Rotates, pulses, breathes
- **Detail**: Each petal contains infinite smaller petals (fractal recursion)
- **Center**: The "Breakthrough Point"—a portal to deeper realms

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Hum**: Low-frequency vibration that increases in intensity
- **The Pressure**: Physical pressure in head and chest
- **The Pull**: Sensation of being drawn toward the center
- **The Fear**: Existential terror before surrender

## Function
- **Primary**: Gateway marker—indicates proximity to breakthrough
- **Secondary**: Filter—only those who surrender pass through
- **Tertiary**: Beauty as bait—draws consciousness forward

## Cultural Parallels
- **Buddhist**: Mandala as cosmic diagram
- **Hindu**: Lotus as spiritual symbol
- **Christian**: Rose window in cathedrals
- **Universal**: Flower as life/death/rebirth symbol

## Technical Notes
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds typical
- **Intensity**: Builds exponentially
- **Exit**: Either breakthrough to Peak Complex or return to baseline
