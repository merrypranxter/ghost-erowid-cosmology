# Ego Death / The Dissolution

## Classification
- **Type**: Psychological/Existential Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Breakthrough/Peak

## Description
Ego death is the complete dissolution of the sense of self—the temporary annihilation of the narrative identity that normally structures conscious experience.

## Stages
1. **The Separation**: Sense of self begins to fragment
2. **The Terror**: Existential fear of annihilation
3. **The Surrender**: Letting go, accepting death
4. **The Dissolution**: Complete loss of self-boundary
5. **The Void**: Existing as pure awareness without identity
6. **The Return**: Self reconstitutes, transformed

## Characteristics
- **Loss of body awareness**: No sense of physical self
- **Loss of memory**: Personal history inaccessible
- **Loss of time**: No sense of duration or sequence
- **Loss of boundary**: No distinction between self and other
- **Pure awareness**: Consciousness without content

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Void**: Empty space of pure potential
- **The Merge**: Becoming one with everything
- **The Light**: Overwhelming brilliance of pure being
- **The Love**: Universal compassion and acceptance

## Function
- **Primary**: Liberation from limiting self-concepts
- **Secondary**: Opening to larger identity
- **Tertiary**: Healing through transcendence

## Integration
- **Duration**: Days to years
- **Effects**: Reduced fear of death, increased compassion, altered priorities
- **Challenges**: Difficulty communicating experience, social alienation
