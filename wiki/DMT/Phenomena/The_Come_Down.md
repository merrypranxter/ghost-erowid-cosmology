# The Come Down / Return Journey

## Classification
- **Type**: Transitional Phenomenon
- **Phase**: DMT Offset

## Description
The Come Down is the return from the DMT space to baseline consciousness—a gradual reintegration that can be as profound as the peak experience.

## Stages
1. **The Ejection**: Sudden or gradual departure from DMT space
2. **The Fade**: Visuals diminish, entities depart
3. **The Return**: Coming back to body awareness
4. **The Afterglow**: Lingering effects, insights
5. **The Integration**: Processing, understanding
6. **The Baseline**: Return to normal consciousness

## Characteristics
- **Speed**: 5-10 minutes typically
- **Clarity**: Increasing mental clarity
- **Emotion**: Often tearful, grateful, awed
- **Physical**: Return of body sensations
- **Memory**: Attempting to hold onto experience

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Ejection**: Sense of being "kicked out"
- **Shadow-Nurses**: Comfort during return
- **The Afterglow**: Hours of enhanced mood
- **The Urge to Share**: Wanting to tell others

## Challenges
- **Amnesia**: Experience fading quickly
- **Frustration**: Can't hold onto insights
- **Disorientation**: Re-adjusting to normal reality
- **Longing**: Wanting to return

## Integration Practices
- **Journaling**: Write down immediately
- **Drawing**: Visual capture
- **Sharing**: Tell a trusted person
- **Rest**: Allow time to process
- **Meditation**: Deepen understanding

## The Afterglow
- **Duration**: Hours to days
- **Effects**: Enhanced mood, clarity, openness
- **Window**: Opportunity for integration
- **Fading**: Gradual return to baseline
