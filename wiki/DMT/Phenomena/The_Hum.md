# The Hum / The Carrier Wave

## Classification
- **Type**: Auditory Phenomenon
- **Phase**: All phases

## Description
The Hum is the fundamental carrier wave of the DMT experience—a low-frequency vibration that underlies all other phenomena and serves as the "soundtrack" to the journey.

## Characteristics
- **Frequency**: 40-80 Hz range (theta-delta border)
- **Quality**: Deep, resonant, organic
- **Source**: Seems to come from everywhere and nowhere
- **Intensity**: Builds from subtle to overwhelming

## Variations
- **The Didgeridoo**: Deep, breathy, pulsing
- **The Bell**: Clear, ringing, crystalline
- **The Choir**: Multiple voices in harmony
- **The Machine**: Mechanical, rhythmic, industrial

## Function
- **Primary**: Carrier wave for information
- **Secondary**: Entrainment of brainwaves
- **Tertiary**: Navigation beacon

## Associated Phenomena
- **Physical vibration**: Body resonates with frequency
- **Visual sync**: Patterns pulse in time with hum
- **Emotional modulation**: Frequency affects emotional state

## Technical Notes
- **Brainwave correlation**: Theta-delta range associated with deep meditation
- **Binaural potential**: May be related to binaural beat effects
- **Universal report**: Nearly all operatives report some form of hum
