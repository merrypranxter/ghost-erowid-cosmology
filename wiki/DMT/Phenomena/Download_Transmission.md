# Download Transmission

## Classification
- **Type**: Information Transfer Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Peak Experience

## Description
The Download is the sudden, overwhelming influx of information that occurs during deep DMT experiences—often triggered by eye contact with Observer-class entities or breakthrough to the Core.

## Characteristics
- **Speed**: Instantaneous—vast amounts in zero time
- **Volume**: More information than could be learned in a lifetime
- **Format**: Non-verbal, direct knowing
- **Retention**: Variable—some lost upon return

## Content Types
- **Mathematical**: Equations, proofs, geometric theorems
- **Historical**: Vast sweeps of cosmic history
- **Technical**: How reality works, mechanics of consciousness
- **Personal**: Insights about one's own life and patterns
- **Universal**: The nature of existence, meaning, purpose

## Physical Correlates
- **Forehead pressure**: Intense pressure at third eye
- **Body temperature**: Heat waves, sweating
- **Heart rate**: Acceleration
- **Breathing**: Shallow, rapid or suspended

## Post-Download
- **Integration**: Takes days, weeks, months, years
- **Amnesia**: Much content lost, fragments remain
- **Transformation**: Permanent changes in worldview
- **Urgency**: Desire to share, communicate, create

## Technical Notes
- **Bandwidth**: Estimated at terabits per second
- **Compression**: Information is highly compressed
- **Decompression**: Occurs over time during integration
