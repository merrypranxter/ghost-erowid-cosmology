# The Library / The Akashic Archive

## Classification
- **Type**: Knowledge Zone
- **Layer**: Archive Layer

## Description
The Library is the infinite archive containing all knowledge from all times—a space where information exists as living, breathing entities that can be accessed, read, and understood directly.

## Structural Features
- **Scale**: Infinite in all directions
- **Shelves**: Extend to impossible heights, curve with non-Euclidean geometry
- **Books**: Living documents that rewrite themselves based on reader
- **Lighting**: Soft, warm, sourceless
- **Atmosphere**: Hushed reverence, like a sacred space

## Book Types
- **Life Books**: Complete records of individual lives
- **Knowledge Books**: Specific domains of information
- **Future Books**: Probabilistic futures, constantly shifting
- **Pattern Books**: The underlying mathematics of reality

## Access Methods
- **Reading**: Direct absorption of information
- **Conversation**: Books can speak their contents
- **Merge**: Become the book, experience its contents directly

## Associated Entities
- The Archivists, The Great Race of Yith

## Function
- **Primary**: Universal knowledge storage
- **Secondary**: Cross-temporal information access
- **Tertiary**: Pattern recognition and understanding

## Cosmological Significance
- **Function**: The memory of the universe
- **Purpose**: All information preserved and accessible
- **Insight**: Knowledge is alive and evolving
