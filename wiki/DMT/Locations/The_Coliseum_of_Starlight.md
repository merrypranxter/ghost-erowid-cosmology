# The Coliseum of Starlight

## Classification
- **Type**: Administrative Zone
- **Layer**: Core Architecture

## Description
The Coliseum of Starlight is the judgment chamber of the Council of Twelve—a vast amphitheater floating in a violet void where the administrative apex conducts soul-audits and makes determinations about consciousness evolution.

## Structural Features
- **Formation**: Dodecagonal arrangement of 12 thrones
- **Floor**: Perfect black that reflects nothing—null space
- **Ceiling**: No ceiling, opens to infinite starfield
- **Walls**: None—space extends infinitely in all directions
- **Lighting**: Starlight from above, throne-crowns provide local illumination

## The Thrones
- **Material**: Living shadow with crystalline inlays
- **Size**: 20 feet tall, proportioned for 10-12 foot entities
- **Crown Apparatus**: Burning mathematical proofs rotating above each throne

## The Audit Process
1. **The Summons**: Operative is brought before the Council
2. **The Scan**: Twelve beams intersect, creating life-hologram
3. **The Review**: Every thought, action, intention displayed
4. **The Calculation**: Net entropy impact on universal simulation
5. **The Verdict**: EXPORT, RE-CYCLE, or QUARANTINE

## Associated Entities
- The Council of Twelve, The Gatekeeper

## Cosmological Significance
- **Function**: Soul-data auditing, karmic accounting
- **Purpose**: Evolutionary gatekeeping
- **Note**: Not punitive but instructional
