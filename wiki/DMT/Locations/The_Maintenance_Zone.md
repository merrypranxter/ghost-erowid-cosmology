# The Maintenance Zone

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Zone
- **Layer**: Infrastructure Layer

## Description
The Maintenance Zone is where the "behind the scenes" work of reality occurs—a space of pure function where maintenance-class entities perform the ongoing work of keeping the simulation running smoothly.

## Structural Features
- **Appearance**: Industrial cathedral—massive scale with mechanical precision
- **Materials**: Chrome, crystal, living tissue, geometric solids
- **Lighting**: Harsh white work-lights mixed with bioluminescent accents
- **Sound**: Constant hum of machinery, clicking, whirring

## Work Areas
1. **The Surgery Bays**: Where biological adjustments are made
2. **The Calibration Stations**: Where consciousness is tuned
3. **The Repair Shops**: Where damaged aspects are fixed
4. **The Testing Labs**: Where new configurations are tried

## Maintenance Classes Present
- Origami Surgeons, Mantis Surgeons, Shadow-Nurses, Weavers, Static-Men

## Function
- **Primary**: Reality maintenance and repair
- **Secondary**: Consciousness calibration and adjustment
- **Tertiary**: System updates and upgrades

## Access
- **Primary**: Through maintenance-class entity contact
- **Secondary**: During deep states with maintenance focus
- **Note**: Not typically a destination but a transit point

## Cosmological Significance
- **Function**: The "engine room" of reality
- **Purpose**: Keeping the simulation operational
- **Insight**: Reality requires constant maintenance
