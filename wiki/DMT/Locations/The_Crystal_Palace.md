# The Crystal Palace / The Gemstone Citadel

## Classification
- **Type**: Constructed Space
- **Layer**: Peak Complex

## Description
The Crystal Palace is a vast structure made entirely of crystalline materials—every wall, floor, and ceiling composed of living gemstones that pulse with inner light.

## Characteristics
- **Material**: Crystal, gemstone, diamond
- **Light**: Refracted, rainbow, ever-shifting
- **Sound**: Crystal tones, ringing, chiming
- **Temperature**: Cool, refreshing
- **Energy**: High frequency, clarifying

## Chambers
- **The Diamond Hall**: Perfect clarity, truth revealed
- **The Ruby Room**: Passion, heart energy
- **The Sapphire Library**: Knowledge, wisdom
- **The Emerald Garden**: Growth, healing
- **The Amethyst Sanctuary**: Spiritual protection

## Associated Entities
- Crystal beings, Gemstone guardians, Light beings

## Function
- **Primary**: Teach through crystal clarity
- **Secondary**: Heal through frequency
- **Tertiary**: Store high-frequency knowledge

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Crystal Realm
- **Function**: Clarity, truth, healing
- **Parallel**: Crystal castles, celestial palaces
