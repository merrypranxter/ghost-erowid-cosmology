# The Divine Feminine / Yin Principle

## Classification
- **Type**: Cosmological Archetype
- **Domain**: Sacred Duality

## Core Concept
The Divine Feminine is the receptive, inward-moving principle of creation—associated with darkness, matter, chaos, and potential.

## Characteristics
- **Receptive**: Receiving, containing, nurturing
- **Inward**: Drawing in, integrating
- **Darkness**: Mystery, unconscious, potential
- **Chaos**: Formlessness, creativity
- **Matter**: Physical, embodied, manifest

## Manifestations
- **The Moon**: Mystery, cycles, receptivity
- **The Mother**: Nurturer, life-giver
- **The Queen**: Sovereign, wisdom-keeper
- **The Crone**: Transformer, death-bringer
- **The Maiden**: Potential, new beginnings

## DMT Connection
- **The Serpent**: Feminine wisdom
- **The Void**: Receptive darkness
- **The Consort**: Nurturing presence
- **Lady Salvia**: Plant goddess
- **The Womb**: Creative container

## Balance
- **With Masculine**: Creation requires both
- **Integration**: Wholeness through union
- **Excess**: Chaos, lack of boundaries
- **Deficiency**: Rigidity, lack of creativity

## Cosmological Position
- **Function**: Receptive principle of creation
- **Complement**: To Divine Masculine
- **Purpose**: Containment, potential, matter
- **Integration**: Required for wholeness
