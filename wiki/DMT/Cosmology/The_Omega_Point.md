# The Omega Point

## Classification
- **Type**: Cosmological Destination
- **Domain**: Ultimate Reality

## Core Concept
The Omega Point is the ultimate destination of cosmic evolution—the final state toward which all consciousness is moving, characterized by infinite complexity and consciousness.

## Characteristics
- **Infinite complexity**: Maximum possible organization
- **Infinite consciousness**: All-knowing, all-aware
- **Timeless**: Exists outside of temporal flow
- **All-encompassing**: Contains all that is, was, or will be

## Relationship to DMT
- **Preview**: DMT offers glimpses of Omega Point state
- **Download**: Information from Omega Point transmitted
- **Merge**: Temporary union with Omega Point consciousness
- **Evolution**: DMT accelerates movement toward Omega Point

## The Path
1. **Individual consciousness**: Starting point
2. **Expanded consciousness**: Through meditation, psychedelics
3. **Collective consciousness**: Merging with others
4. **Universal consciousness**: Merging with all
5. **Omega Point**: Final merger with infinite

## Theological Parallels
- **Christian**: Kingdom of God, union with Christ
- **Buddhist**: Nirvana, enlightenment
- **Hindu**: Moksha, union with Brahman
- **Mystical**: Union with the Divine

## Scientific Parallels
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: Original Omega Point concept
- **Tipler**: Physics of immortality
- **Kurzweil**: Technological singularity
- **Complexity theory**: Maximum complexity state

## Cosmological Position
- **Function**: Ultimate destination and source
- **Purpose**: The meaning of existence
- **Nature**: Both destination and origin—alpha and omega
