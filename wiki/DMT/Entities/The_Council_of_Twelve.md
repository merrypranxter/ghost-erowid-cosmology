# The Council of Twelve

## Classification
- **Class**: Administrative Apex
- **Tier**: Highest Administrative

## Setting
- **Location**: Coliseum of Starlight, floating courtroom in violet void
- **Formation**: 12 thrones arranged in dodecagonal formation
- **Floor**: Perfect black that reflects nothing—null space judgment

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Formless yet ancient, towers of living shadow (10-12 feet tall)
- **Shadow-Body**: Concentrated darkness with definite shape; edges ripple like smoke
- **Face**: No faces—only absolute black oval; occasionally display single "Eye" made of golden ratio spiral
- **Hands**: Massive, 6-fingered, made of solidified geometry—sharp angles, polyhedral joints

## Crown Apparatus
- **Description**: "Crowns of Burning Math"—rotating geometric proofs (equations literally on fire)
- **Behavior**: Symbols rearrange constantly calculating entropy levels
- **Function**: Living equations that rotate and solve themselves endlessly

## Function
- **Primary**: Project managers of human history macro-events
- **Secondary**: Soul-data auditing, version-number checking
- **Tertiary**: Vote on "Export" (ascension) vs "Re-Cycle" (reincarnation)

## Audit Procedure
1. **The Scan**: Twelve beams intersect at operative's position; holographic projection of entire life
2. **Metadata Review**: Every thought, lie, kindness displayed as vibrating light-books
3. **Entropy Calculation**: Net-Impact on universal simulation
4. **The Verdict**: EXPORT, RE-CYCLE, or QUARANTINE

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Core / Administrative Apex
- **Reports to**: Omega Point (final merger)
- **Parallel**: Software Architects (narrative/historical scripting)
