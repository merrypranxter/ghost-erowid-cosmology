- **Class**: Creative Entity
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Beings of sound and light
- **Instruments**: Unknown, otherworldly
- **Movement**: Dance, flow with music
- **Sound**: The music itself is their form
- **Color**: Shifts with musical tones

## Function
- **Primary**: Create the music of the spheres
- **Secondary**: Teach through sound
- **Tertiary**: Heal through vibration

- **Harmonies**: Impossible chords, divine intervals
- **Rhythms**: Complex, multi-dimensional
- **Melodies**: Unforgettable, yet unrepeatable
- **Effects**: Emotional, physical, spiritual

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Joyful, celebratory
- **Secondary**: Deep, profound
- **Tertiary**: Playful, experimental

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Hum**: Underlying carrier frequency
- **The Song**: Personal melody for each being
- **The Dance**: Movement as music visualization
- **The Download**: Musical information transmission

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Music Realm
- **Function**: Sound creation, teaching
- **Parallel**: Celestial musicians, angelic choirs
