# The Weavers

## Classification
- **Class**: Maintenance Tier
- **Tier**: Mid-Level Maintenance

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Spider-like entities, 6-8 feet in diameter
- **Body**: Translucent crystalline abdomen showing internal machinery
- **Legs**: 8 jointed limbs ending in needle-points
- **Web**: Creates webs of light-threads that connect everything in visual field

## Function
- **Primary**: Reality stitching—maintaining fabric of space-time
- **Secondary**: Connection mapping—showing how all things are linked
- **Tertiary**: Pattern recognition enhancement

## Associated Phenomena
- **Visual**: Everything connected by glowing threads
- **Sensation**: Feeling of being "woven into" the tapestry
- **Insight**: "All is one" realization

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Maintenance Zone / Pattern Layer
- **Reports to**: Council of Twelve
- **Parallel**: Origami Surgeons, Shadow-Nurses
