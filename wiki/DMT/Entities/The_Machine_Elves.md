# The Machine Elves / Tyktes

## Classification
- **Class**: Trickster/Daemon Class
- **Tier**: Heuristic Daemons

## Physical Description
- **Size**: 1-2 feet tall (gnome-scale) but can expand to human size
- **Form**: "Self-transforming machine-basketballs"—spherical core with external limbs
- **Material**: Liquid silver and rainbow chrome
- **Surface**: Hyper-kinetic—constantly shifting between smooth sphere and spiky star-shape
- **Limbs**: 4-8 pipe-cleaner-like arms, jointless, bend in curves
- **Hands**: Too many fingers (9-12 per hand) that wiggle constantly
- **Face**: Huge grinning mouth (50% of face), eyes are spinning hypno-discs or LED-matrix displays

## Movement
- **Velocity**: "Infinite Velocity"—move faster than eye can track
- **Style**: Bounce rather than walk, leaving "motion lines" (cartoon speed lines)

## Behavior
- **Urgency**: Frantic, hyperactive; "Look! Look at this!"
- **Demonstration**: Creating impossible objects from "nothing"
- **Purpose**: Not teaching but reminding—"Look what you forgot you could do!"
- **Anamnesis**: Attempting to trigger unforgetting

## Function
- **Primary**: Background creative daemons
- **Secondary**: Demo reality-engine (heuristic daemons)
- **Tertiary**: Beta-testing, chaos injection

## Response Patterns
- **If ignored**: Increase intensity—more objects, faster movement, louder colors
- **If resisted**: Can become overwhelming (sensory overload)
- **Success indicator**: Elves cheer, grant deeper access

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Peak Complex / The Nursery
- **Reports to**: None (autonomous processes)
- **Parallel**: The Great Race of Yith (archivists, technicians)
