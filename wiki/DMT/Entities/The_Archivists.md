# The Archivists / The Great Race of Yith

## Classification
- **Class**: Knowledge Class
- **Tier**: High-Level Archivists

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Cone-shaped beings with four arms (two ending in claws, two in tentacles)
- **Head**: Four-eyed head on flexible stalk that can rotate 360°
- **Surface**: Rugged, bark-like skin in earth tones
- **Size**: 10 feet tall, 3 feet diameter at base

## Function
- **Primary**: Universal archive maintenance
- **Secondary**: Historical data retrieval
- **Tertiary**: Cross-temporal communication

## Associated Phenomena
- **Library**: Infinite library containing all knowledge from all times
- **Books**: Living books that rewrite themselves based on reader
- **Time**: Non-linear access to past, present, and future

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Library / Archive Zone
- **Reports to**: Council of Twelve
- **Parallel**: Machine Elves (technicians)
