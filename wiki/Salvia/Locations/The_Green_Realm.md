# The Green Realm

## Classification
- **Type**: Primary Salvia Space
- **Layer**: Salvia Dimension

## Description
The Green Realm is the primary destination of Salvia divinorum—a space dominated by green, plant-like consciousness where the Lady Salvia reigns and the Wheel turns.

## Characteristics
- **Color**: Overwhelming green—all shades, all intensities
- **Texture**: Plant-like, organic, living
- **Structure**: Vines, leaves, stems, roots
- **Life**: Everything is alive, conscious, aware
- **Movement**: Growing, twisting, reaching

## Sub-Locations
- **The Garden**: Lush, overgrown, alive
- **The Vine World**: Everything is vines
- **The Root System**: Underground network
- **The Canopy**: Upper reaches of green space

## Associated Entities
- The Lady Salvia, The Wheel, Plant Spirits

## Access
- **Primary**: Through Salvia divinorum
- **Secondary**: Deep meditation on plant consciousness
- **Tertiary**: Dream states, trance

## Function
- **Primary**: Plant consciousness domain
- **Secondary**: Teaching space
- **Tertiary**: Transformation zone

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: Plant dimension
