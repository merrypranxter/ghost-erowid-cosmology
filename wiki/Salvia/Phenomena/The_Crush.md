# The Crush / The Grind

## Classification
- **Type**: Somatic/Existential Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Peak Salvia

## Description
The Crush is the overwhelming physical and psychological sensation of being crushed, ground, or compressed by the Wheel or other mechanism.

## Characteristics
- **Physical**: Sensation of pressure, squeezing
- **Psychological**: Ego dissolution, loss of self
- **Existential**: Sense of annihilation
- **Mechanical**: Impersonal, inevitable

## Stages
1. **Approach**: Sense of something coming
2. **Contact**: First pressure, resistance
3. **Full Crush**: Overwhelming pressure, surrender
4. **Grind**: Old self broken down
5. **Release**: New self emerges

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Wheel**: Massive grinding mechanism
- **The Grinders**: Entities doing the crushing
- **The Reform**: Being reshaped
- **Ego death**: Loss of identity

## Function
- **Primary**: Break down old patterns
- **Secondary**: Prepare for transformation
- **Tertiary**: Ego dissolution

## Integration
- **Surrender**: Fighting increases suffering
- **Trust**: The process is beneficial
- **Temporary**: It will pass
- **Transformative**: Results in positive change
