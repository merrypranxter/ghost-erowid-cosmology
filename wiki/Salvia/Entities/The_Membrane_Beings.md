# The Membrane Beings / The Folded Ones

## Classification
- **Class**: Dimensional Entities
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Flat, 2D beings existing in folded space
- **Surface**: Like paper cutouts, living diagrams
- **Movement**: Sliding along folded surfaces
- **Perception**: Can see "around" corners of 3D space

## Function
- **Primary**: Inhabitants of the folded dimensions
- **Secondary**: Demonstrate the true nature of space
- **Tertiary**: Assist in crossing dimensional boundaries

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Fold**: Space folding like origami
- **The Page**: Reality as page being turned
- **The Edge**: Seeing the "edge" of 3D space
- **The Zipper**: Unzipping the fabric of reality

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Neutral, curious, observing
- **Secondary**: Helpful, guiding
- **Tertiary**: Alien, incomprehensible

## The Folded World
- **2D existence**: Life on flat surfaces
- **3D perception**: Can see into our dimension
- **4D access**: Can move through folded space
- **The crease**: Where dimensions meet

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Folded Dimension / The Membrane
- **Function**: Dimensional inhabitants, guides
- **Parallel**: Flatland beings, dimensional theory

## Interaction Notes
- **Disorienting**: Their perspective challenges 3D thinking
- **Revealing**: Show the constructed nature of space
- **Playful**: Often amused by 3D confusion
- **Teaching**: Demonstrate dimensional concepts directly
