# The Background People / The Extras

## Classification
- **Class**: NPC Entities
- **Tier**: Background

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Human-like but generic
- **Features**: Vague, shifting, not fully formed
- **Behavior**: Going about routine activities
- **Awareness**: Seem unaware of operative

## Function
- **Primary**: Populate the background of reality
- **Secondary**: Demonstrate the "set" of existence
- **Tertiary**: Show that reality is populated

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Set**: Reality as stage set
- **The Script**: Life as scripted performance
- **The Extras**: Background characters

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Neutral, background
- **Secondary**: Slightly eerie
- **Tertiary**: Revealing of constructed nature

- **Repetitive**: Same actions, loops
- **Unaware**: Don't respond to operative

## Cosmological Position
