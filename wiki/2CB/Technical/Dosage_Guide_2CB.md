# 2C-B Dosage Guide

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Reference
- **Domain**: Practical Information

## Threshold (2-5 mg)
- **Effects**: Subtle mood lift, mild body awareness
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours
- **Use**: Microdosing, sensitivity testing

## Light (5-10 mg)
- **Effects**: Mild visuals, body high
- **Duration**: 3-5 hours
- **Use**: Beginner exploration, social

## Common (10-20 mg)
- **Effects**: Strong visuals, enhanced empathy
- **Duration**: 4-6 hours
- **Use**: Standard experience

## Strong (20-30 mg)
- **Effects**: Intense visuals, strong body load
- **Duration**: 5-8 hours
- **Use**: Experienced practitioners

## Heavy (30+ mg)
- **Effects**: Overwhelming, ego dissolution possible
- **Duration**: 6-10 hours
- **Use**: Advanced, with preparation

## Administration Methods
- **Oral**: Most common, 2-4 hour onset
- **Insufflation**: Faster onset, more intense, shorter
- **Rectal**: Efficient, moderate onset

## Body Load
- **Common**: Nausea, muscle tension
- **Management**: Movement, breathing, acceptance
- **Positive**: Can be pleasurable, sensual
- **Dose correlation**: More pronounced at higher doses

## Safety Considerations
- **Set and setting**: Important for positive experience
- **Sitter**: Recommended for higher doses
- **Heart conditions**: Use caution
- **MAOIs**: Dangerous interaction
- **Testing**: Test for purity

## Unique Characteristics
- **Empathogenic**: MDMA-like empathy
- **Psychedelic**: LSD-like visuals
- **Sensual**: Enhanced touch, sexuality
- **Clear-headed**: Less mental fog than other psychedelics

## Integration
- **Journaling**: Record insights
- **Rest**: Allow time to process
- **Relationships**: Deepen connections
- **Creativity**: Enhanced artistic expression
