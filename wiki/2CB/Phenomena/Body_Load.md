# Body Load / Somatic Effects

## Classification
- **Type**: Somatic Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Onset/Peak 2CB

## Description
Body load refers to the physical sensations and somatic effects of 2CB—often more pronounced than other psychedelics, with a distinct "body high."

## Characteristics
- **Intensity**: Can be strong, sometimes uncomfortable
- **Location**: Often focused in torso, gut
- **Quality**: Energetic, buzzing, warm
- **Duration**: Varies, often through peak

## Common Manifestations
- **Nausea**: Common on come-up
- **Energy**: Surges of bodily energy
- **Temperature**: Fluctuations, sweating
- **Tension**: Muscle tightness, jaw clenching
- **Sensitivity**: Enhanced body awareness

## Positive Aspects
- **Pleasure**: Can be deeply pleasurable
- **Sensitivity**: Enhanced touch, sensation
- **Presence**: Deep embodiment
- **Energy**: Available for movement, dance

## Challenging Aspects
- **Nausea**: Can be significant
- **Discomfort**: Restlessness, unease
- **Tension**: Physical holding patterns
- **Overwhelm**: Too much sensation

## Management
- **Movement**: Dance, stretch, walk
- **Breath**: Deep breathing, relaxation
- **Touch**: Massage, contact
- **Acceptance**: Allow, don't fight
- **Setting**: Comfortable, safe space
