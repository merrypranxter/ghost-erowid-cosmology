# The Love Garden / The Heart Sanctuary

## Classification
- **Type**: Emotional Space
- **Layer**: Heart Dimension

## Description
The Love Garden is a space of pure emotional openness and connection—where love flows freely between all beings and the environment itself radiates affection.

## Characteristics
- **Atmosphere**: Thick with love, palpable warmth
- **Colors**: Pinks, reds, golds—heart colors
- **Plants**: Flowers that pulse with love
- **Beings**: Love Beings inhabit this space
- **Music**: Heart music, love songs of the universe

## Features
- **The Heart Fountain**: Source of flowing love
- **The Embrace Grove**: Trees that hug and hold
- **The Connection Meadow**: Open space for meeting
- **The Intimacy Garden**: Private spaces for deep connection

## Associated Entities
- Love Beings, Sensual Guides, The Beloved

## Function
- **Primary**: Experience unconditional love
- **Secondary**: Heal heart wounds
- **Tertiary**: Learn love as fundamental force

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Heart Realm
- **Function**: Love sanctuary, healing space
- **Parallel**: Garden of love, Venus's garden
