# Ego Dissolution (LSD)

## Classification
- **Type**: Psychological Phenomenon
- **Phase**: Peak LSD

## Description
Ego dissolution on LSD is the gradual or sudden loss of the sense of separate self—boundaries between self and other, subject and object, dissolve into unity.

## Stages
1. **Loosening**: Boundaries begin to soften
2. **Blurring**: Self/other distinction weakens
3. **Merging**: Sense of becoming one with surroundings
4. **Unity**: Complete dissolution into oneness
5. **Integration**: Return with expanded sense of self

## Characteristics
- **Boundary loss**: Skin no longer feels like edge of self
- **Unity**: Feeling one with everything
- **Timelessness**: Present moment expands
- **Love**: Universal compassion arises
- **Awe**: Overwhelming wonder

## Associated Phenomena
- **Oceanic boundlessness**: Feeling of infinite expansion
- **Cosmic consciousness**: Awareness of universal mind
- **Mystical union**: Merge with divine/transcendent
- **Satori**: Sudden enlightenment

## Function
- **Primary**: Transcend limited self-concept
- **Secondary**: Experience universal connection
- **Tertiary**: Healing through expanded identity

## Integration
- **Lasting effects**: Reduced ego rigidity
- **Increased empathy**: Greater connection to others
- **Spiritual insight**: Deeper understanding of unity
- **Behavioral change**: More compassionate action
