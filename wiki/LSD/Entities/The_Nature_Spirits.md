# The Nature Spirits / The Elementals

## Classification
- **Class**: Nature Entities
- **Tier**: Mid-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Varies—plant, animal, elemental
- **Plant**: Tree people, flower beings, grass spirits
- **Animal**: Animal guides, totemic beings
- **Elemental**: Earth, air, fire, water spirits
- **Material**: Natural materials, living matter

## Function
- **Primary**: Guardians of nature
- **Secondary**: Teachers of natural wisdom
- **Tertiary**: Healers through nature connection

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Ancient, wise, patient
- **Secondary**: Playful, wild, free
- **Tertiary**: Fierce, protective, warning

## Associated Phenomena
- **Nature connection**: Deep bond with natural world
- **Plant consciousness**: Awareness in all life
- **Animal communication**: Understanding with creatures
- **Elemental power**: Connection to forces of nature

## The Nature Connection
- **Trees**: Ancient wisdom, rooted presence
- **Flowers**: Beauty, opening, pollination
- **Animals**: Instinct, guidance, medicine
- **Elements**: Fundamental forces

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Natural World
- **Function**: Nature guardians and teachers
- **Parallel**: Elementals, nature spirits, faeries
