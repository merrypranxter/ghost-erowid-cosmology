# The Light Beings / The Luminous Ones

## Classification
- **Class**: Luminous Entities
- **Tier**: High-Level

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Beings of pure light, no fixed shape
- **Color**: White, gold, rainbow spectrum
- **Brightness**: Intense but not blinding
- **Movement**: Flowing, dancing, pulsing
- **Boundary**: Edges shimmer, undefined

## Function
- **Primary**: Guides, teachers, healers
- **Secondary**: Sources of wisdom and love
- **Tertiary**: Facilitators of transformation

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Loving, welcoming, accepting
- **Secondary**: Wise, ancient, knowing
- **Tertiary**: Playful, joyful, celebratory

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Light**: Overwhelming luminosity
- **The Love**: Universal compassion
- **The Download**: Information transmission
- **The Merge**: Union with light

## Communication
- **Telepathic**: Direct mind-to-mind
- **Emotional**: Through feeling, not words
- **Visual**: Through imagery, symbolism
- **Being**: Through presence alone

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Light Realm
- **Function**: Guides and teachers
- **Parallel**: Angels, light beings, ascended masters
