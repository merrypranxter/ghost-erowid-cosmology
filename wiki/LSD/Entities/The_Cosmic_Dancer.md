# The Cosmic Dancer / Shiva Nataraja

## Classification
- **Class**: Creative/Destructive Entity
- **Tier**: Apex

## Physical Description
- **Form**: Dancer in cosmic dance
- **Movement**: Constant, flowing, precise
- **Expression**: Joy and concentration
- **Fire**: Ring of flame surrounding
- **Multiple arms**: Many gestures simultaneously

## Function
- **Primary**: Creation and destruction through dance
- **Secondary**: Teach the dance of existence
- **Tertiary**: Liberation through movement

- **Creation**: Each step creates worlds
- **Destruction**: Each step dissolves worlds
- **Preservation**: The dance maintains existence
- **Liberation**: Dance as path to freedom

## Affect Profile
- **Primary**: Joyful, concentrated
- **Secondary**: Fierce, powerful
- **Tertiary**: Playful, mischievous

## Associated Phenomena
- **The Rhythm**: Underlying beat of universe
- **The Movement**: All existence as dance
- **The Fire**: Transformation, purification
- **The Still Point**: Center of the dance

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Dance Floor / Cosmic Stage
- **Function**: Creation through movement
