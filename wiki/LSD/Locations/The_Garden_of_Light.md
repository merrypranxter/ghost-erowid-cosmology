# The Garden of Light

## Classification
- **Type**: Natural Space
- **Layer**: Luminous Dimension

## Description
The Garden of Light is a paradise-like natural space suffused with divine light—every plant, flower, and creature glows with inner radiance.

## Characteristics
- **Light**: Everything glows from within
- **Plants**: Luminous flowers, light-trees
- **Creatures**: Glowing animals, light-beings
- **Atmosphere**: Soft, warm, loving
- **Colors**: Pastel, rainbow, ever-shifting

## Features
- **The Light Trees**: Trees of pure radiance
- **The Flower Fields**: Infinite glowing flowers
- **The River of Light**: Flowing stream of liquid light
- **The Heart Center**: Most intense love and light

## Associated Entities
- Light Beings, Nature Spirits, The Beloved

## Function
- **Primary**: Healing through love and light
- **Secondary**: Teaching through beauty
- **Tertiary**: Rest and restoration

## Cosmological Position
- **Layer**: The Light Realm
- **Function**: Paradise, healing space
- **Parallel**: Garden of Eden, Pure Land
