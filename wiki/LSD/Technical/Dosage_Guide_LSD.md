# LSD Dosage Guide

## Classification
- **Type**: Technical Reference
- **Domain**: Practical Information

## Microdose (5-20 µg)
- **Effects**: Subtle mood lift, enhanced focus
- **Duration**: 4-6 hours
- **Use**: Creativity, productivity, mood

## Threshold (20-40 µg)
- **Effects**: Mild visuals, mood enhancement
- **Duration**: 6-8 hours
- **Use**: Social, light exploration

## Light (40-75 µg)
- **Effects**: Clear visuals, altered thinking
- **Duration**: 8-10 hours
- **Use**: Beginner full experience

## Common (75-150 µg)
- **Effects**: Strong visuals, ego softening
- **Duration**: 10-12 hours
- **Use**: Standard psychedelic experience

## Strong (150-300 µg)
- **Effects**: Intense visuals, ego dissolution
- **Duration**: 12-16 hours
- **Use**: Experienced practitioners

## Heavy (300+ µg)
- **Effects**: Complete dissolution, mystical
- **Duration**: 16+ hours
- **Use**: Advanced, with preparation

## Administration
- **Blotter**: Most common, paper tabs
- **Liquid**: Precise dosing, versatile
- **Microdots**: Small pills
- **Gel tabs**: Gelatin matrix

## Safety Considerations
- **Set and setting**: Crucial for positive experience
- **Sitter**: Recommended for higher doses
- **Duration**: Long commitment, plan accordingly
- **Heart conditions**: Use caution
- **Mental health**: Caution with history of psychosis
- **Testing**: Test for purity, avoid NBOMe

## Integration
- **Time**: Allow full next day for rest
- **Journaling**: Record insights
- **Support**: Discuss with trusted person
- **Meditation**: Deepen understanding
